Message From MD

Nothing stays the same in today's fast moving world except time tested human values that serves humanity.

Arcus was founded with a commitment to practice those time tested finest human values in every sphere of its operation and in the heart of its culture stays mutual respect and love towards its teammates.We believe that the best way to achieve our vision of becoming the most customer friendly logistics service provider and an employer of choice is to practice and nurture those sublime human values in the organization. We believe only happy and contended teammates can be customer friendly and keep them happy. The name Arcus reflects the amalgamation of finest human values, blend of talents, innovation, customer friendliness, commitment to quality services and professionalism at its height. Arcus Shipping is committed to meet those ever increasing needs of our customers through technology driven solutions, Arcus-i our system, is specially designed to ensure seamless and transparent information flow to our customers. Customer satisfaction will always be at the heart of our operations.We believe that we will be successful only when our customers are successful. At "Arcus" customers are an integral part of our business and we share a warm and cordial relationship with them. By keeping relationships first, we make them lasting. Our local expertise copled with reliable global networks like the WCA,WFN,WWPC,GFP,PAIMA & IAM we make the globe a village and take care of the endless needs of our customers no matter how big or small they are...!

We believe in our tag line "Together everyone achieves more..." and sincerely forward to serve you and and humanity as whole.

Sincerely,

Ali Mohammad Masmali
Managing Director
